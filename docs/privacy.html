Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the AxelBase Timestamp Normalizer, a free, open-source, privacy-first web tool developed by AxelBase ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our tool to convert timestamps into ISO 8601 format. We are committed to your privacy and operate on a zero-data-collection model. The tool runs entirely in your browser — no data is sent to servers, no logs are kept, and no personal information is ever collected.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Timestamp Normalizer is designed to function without gathering any data. We do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, emails, IP addresses, or device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, operating system, location, or session duration.
  • Input Data: Any timestamp you paste (e.g., 11/16/2025 2:19 PM) is processed locally and never transmitted.
  • Analytics or Tracking: No Google Analytics, cookies, pixels, or third-party trackers.

All parsing, conversion, and copy-to-clipboard actions happen client-side only. Your data never leaves your device.

3. How the Tool Works

When you use the Timestamp Normalizer:

  • Input Processing: You enter a timestamp. Luxon parses it using your selected or auto-detected timezone — all in-browser.
  • No Transmission: No data is sent to any server. ISO outputs are generated instantly on your device.
  • Temporary Storage: Only browser memory is used during your session. Data is cleared on page refresh or close.

This client-side architecture ensures complete data isolation — ideal for sensitive logs or internal tools.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any external services that collect data. Open-source libraries (e.g., Luxon) are bundled locally. Copy-to-clipboard uses the native navigator.clipboard API — no third-party involvement.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The tool is completely stateless. We do not use cookies, localStorage, or any tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

Since no data is collected, there is no risk of breach. We recommend:

  • Using the tool over HTTPS (GitHub Pages default)
  • Avoiding input of highly sensitive data in public environments

7. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. International Compliance

By collecting no data, we comply with GDPR, CCPA, POPIA, and global privacy laws by default.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

Your privacy is non-negotiable. Use the Timestamp Normalizer with confidence — your data stays yours.
