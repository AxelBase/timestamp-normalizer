Your First ISO 8601 Conversion

Your First ISO 8601 Conversion

Published: October 22, 2025

Let’s convert your first timestamp — in under 10 seconds. No setup. No login. Just paste and go.

Step 1: Open the Tool

Go to axelbase.github.io/timestamp-normalizer. The interface loads instantly.

Step 2: Paste Your Timestamp

Try any of these:

  • 2025-11-16 14:19
  • 1731761940000
  • 11/16/2025 2:19 PM

The tool auto-detects your timezone (e.g., SAST) and parses instantly.

Step 3: Review Output

You’ll see:

  • Parsed Local Time: 16 November 2025, 14:19:00 SAST
  • ISO UTC: 2025-11-16T12:19:00Z
  • ISO with Offset: 2025-11-16T14:19:00+02:00

Step 4: Copy & Use

Click the Copy button next to either ISO string. Paste into:

  • API payloads
  • Database inserts
  • Documentation
  • Log analysis

Pro Tip: Manual Timezone

Toggle Auto-Detect off to force America/New_York or any IANA zone. Perfect for server logs.

FAQ

What if the input is invalid?

A clear error appears. Fix the format and try again.

Can I convert multiple at once?

Not yet — but batch mode is planned for v2.

One paste. One click. One perfect ISO string. Welcome to the future of timestamp conversion.
